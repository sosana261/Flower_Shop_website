#  Flower World - Online Flower Shop

##  About the Project

"Flower World" is an online flower shop designed to provide users with a smooth, easy, and enjoyable shopping experience. Our website offers a wide variety of flowers for all occasions, with simple browsing, secure purchasing, flexible payment options, and fast delivery services. Customers can also contact us easily via phone or email.

# Features

- User-friendly interface
- Wide selection of flowers for all occasions
- Easy and secure checkout process
- Multiple payment methods
- Fast and reliable delivery service
- Customer support via phone and email

##  How to Purchase

1. Visit the "Flower World" website
2. Go to the Shop page
3. Choose your favorite flowers and click Add to Cart
4. Open your Cart to review your items
5. You can remove products or cancel the order before checkout
6. Enter your details:

   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Delivery Address
